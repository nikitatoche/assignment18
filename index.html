Furniture is any movable object that is used to support various human activities such as seating, sleeping, eating, storage, and decoration.

Seating furniture can be made of different materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and fabric.

Sleeping furniture is designed to provide a comfortable place to sleep and rest.

Storage furniture is designed to provide storage space for various items in homes and offices.

well-planned and designed arrangement of a number of steps provided in a building for vertical movement of people is called stairs.

Outdoor furniture is any type of furniture designed for use outside, such as on a patio, deck, or in garden.
